About Me

I am a researcher specializing in axial vortex in compressible flow. My work focuses on computational fluid dynamics (CFD), OpenFOAM simulations, and vortex-induced flows.

Research Work

  • CFD Analysis of Vortex-Induced Flows: Studying the effects of vortex formation in confined spaces.
  • Computational Modeling of Rotating Cylinders: Analyzing shear stress and viscous forces in rotational flows.
  • OpenFOAM Simulations: Implementing custom solvers for aerospace applications.

Publications

Here are some of my latest research papers:

  • "Axial Vortex Structures in Compressible Flow" - Journal of Fluid Mechanics, 2024
  • "Shear Stress in Rotating Cylinders: A CFD Approach" - AIAA SciTech, 2023
  • "Numerical Analysis of Vortex Breakdown" - Computational Fluid Dynamics Journal, 2022
